Rulebook

Rosters
The field for each tournament will be split into five groups. Groups will be chosen based on the PGC Rating.
Each team chooses 2 golfers from each of the 5 groupings to create your 10 golfer team for the tournament.
If a golfer withdraws prior to the tournament teeing off and you do not edit your team prior to tee off then he will be replaced with the highest world ranked golfer in that group.
Scoring
During Rounds 1 and 2 of the tournament your team’s score will be the average strokes of all 10 golfers on your team.
During Rounds 3 and 4 of the tournament your team’s score will be the average strokes of the 5 lowest golfers on your team that day.
The leaderboard will look just like a PGA leaderboard and tracked live on the Live Leaderboard page.
Tournaments with no cut line will use all 10 golfers for scoring during all 4 rounds of the tournament
Teams must have 5 golfers make the weekend cut line or their team will be cut from the PGC Pools event. Any golfers that withdraw before cut day will receive a score of 8-over par until cut day.
Payouts
Buy-in for each pool is $20. Send your buy-in to coachcartergolf@gmail.com
The top 3% of finishers will win money and payouts will be determined once the tournament has begun and the size of the pool is finalized.

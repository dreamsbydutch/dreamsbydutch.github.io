Welcome to PGC Pools
The tournament field has been split in to 5 groups and you will pick 2 from each group to create your 10 golfer team.
Your team's score will be the average strokes of your entire team during rounds 1 and 2 and the average strokes of just your lowest 5 golfers during rounds 3 and 4.

The leaderboard will be similar to a real PGA leaderboard and updated live. The Pool has a $20 buy-in and the top 3 finishers at the end of the tournament will win a percentage of the pot.

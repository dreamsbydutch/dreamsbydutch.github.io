Welcome to the PGC Tour!
The PGC Tour is a brand new way to play fantasy golf.

Prior to each of the 10 tournaments on the Tour Schedule you will pick your 10 golfer team for that weekend. The tournamanet field will be split in to 5 groupings based on their PGC Rating and you will pick two golfers from each grouping.

Your team's score will be the average strokes of your entire team during rounds 1 and 2 and the average strokes of just your lowest 5 golfers during rounds 3 and 4.

The leaderboard will be similar to a real PGA leaderboard and updated live. The Tour has a $40 buy-in and each tournament is an opportunity to earn both PGC Cup points and actual earnings that will accumulate at the end of the year.
